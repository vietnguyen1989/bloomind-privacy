# Bloomind — Account & Data Deletion

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

## How to delete your account

### Option 1: From the app

1. Open the Bloomind app
2. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation)
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion by entering your password

### Option 2: By email

Send an email to **bloomind.wellness@gmail.com** with the subject line **"Delete My Account"** from the email address associated with your Bloomind account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Account and profile information (name, email)
- All emotion check-ins and mood history
- Journal entries and tags
- Tree progress, XP, and achievements
- Streak history and badges
- Notification preferences
- Subscription data
- Chat conversation history

## What is retained

- Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to you
- Records required for legal or compliance purposes

## Deletion timeline

Your data will be permanently deleted within **30 days** of your request. During this period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible.

## Questions?

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at **bloomind.wellness@gmail.com**.
