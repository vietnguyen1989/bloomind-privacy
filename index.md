# Bloomind Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 17, 2026**

Welcome to Bloomind. Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use the Bloomind mobile application ("App").

> **Summary:** We collect only the data necessary to provide emotional wellness features. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, share it with third-party analytics providers, or track your location.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Email address & password | Account creation and authentication (secured with JWT tokens) |
| Emotional check-in data | Mood ratings and emotion selections to track your emotional wellness over time |
| Journal entries | Free-text personal reflections you choose to record |

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Push notification tokens | Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens used to deliver reminders you have opted into |
| Usage data | Streak tracking and gamification XP to power motivation features within the App |

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location or GPS data
- Contacts or address book
- Photos, camera, or microphone data
- Advertising identifiers
- Data from third-party analytics or tracking SDKs

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the App's emotional wellness features
- Authenticate your account and keep it secure
- Deliver push notification reminders you have opted into
- Track streaks and XP for in-app gamification
- Respond to your support requests or inquiries

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers powered by Google Cloud Run with a PostgreSQL database. We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption in transit** -- all communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS.
- **Encryption at rest** -- data stored in our database is encrypted at rest.
- **Token-based authentication** -- sessions are managed with JSON Web Tokens (JWT); your password is never stored in plain text.
- **Access controls** -- only the developer has administrative access to production systems.

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not**:

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
- Share your data with external analytics providers

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Legal requirements** -- if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
- **Safety** -- if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person or to prevent illegal activity.
- **Infrastructure providers** -- our hosting provider (Google Cloud Platform) processes data on our behalf under strict contractual obligations. Firebase Cloud Messaging is used solely to deliver push notifications.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Portability

You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you by contacting us at the email address below.

### 5.2 Correction

You can update your account information directly within the App. For other corrections, please contact us.

### 5.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To do so, use the account deletion option in the App's settings or email us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

### 5.4 Push Notifications

You can opt out of push notifications at any time through your device settings or the App's notification preferences.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Bloomind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

---

## 8. International Data Transfers

Our servers are hosted on Google Cloud Platform. If you access the App from outside the region where our servers are located, your data may be transferred across international borders. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

---

## 9. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Cloud Platform** (Cloud Run, PostgreSQL) -- infrastructure and data hosting.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** -- push notification delivery only; no analytics or tracking data is sent to Firebase.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
